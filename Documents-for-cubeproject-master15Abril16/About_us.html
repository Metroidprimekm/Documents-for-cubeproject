About us


We are a some students of the Master in Mechatronics at the FH Aachen, in a partnership of CIDESI, a research centre in the city of Querétaro, Mexico and this is our project for the Master. We are a group of students who had developed this Web Applications with the supervision of the Prof. Jörg Wollert.
